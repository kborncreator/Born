GDPR & Privacy

Last updated: June 2026

Artist Positions / Zdeněk Konvalina respects your privacy. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Who we are

Artist Positions is a project by Zdeněk Konvalina, based in Brno, Czech Republic. Contact: konvalina@me.com

What data we collect

We collect only the data you provide directly — your name, email address, shipping address, and any message you submit through our Inquire or Reserve forms. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising networks, or third-party analytics by default.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to respond to your inquiry or process a reservation. It is stored securely and never shared with third parties, sold, or used for marketing without your explicit consent.

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at konvalina@me.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Retention

We retain inquiry and reservation data for as long as necessary to fulfil your request, and no longer than 24 months unless required by law.
